Econometrics Society Special Lectures

  • Fischer-Schulz Lectures The Fisher-Schultz Lecture is an annual lecture given by a non-European member at the European meeting or, in World Congress years, at the World Congress. The lecturer is chosen by the meeting program chairs.

    • Manski (2004), Measuring Expectations
    • Horowitz (2011), Applied Nonparametric Instrumental Variables Estimation
